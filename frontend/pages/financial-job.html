Financial Planning

What is ROI (Return on Investment)?

ROI is a measure of the profitability of your educational investment. It calculates how much return you can expect from the education you receive in relation to the costs of your program.

  • Higher ROI means a higher return on investment, making the profession more financially rewarding.
  • Lower ROI indicates a lower return, meaning the profession may not offer as high a financial benefit relative to the education cost.
