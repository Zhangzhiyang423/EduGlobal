How ROI is calculated

Formula: (5-year expected earnings − total study cost) ÷ total study cost × 100%

What it includes: Total study cost includes tuition fees and your estimated living cost, minus scholarships and part-time income. Expected earnings are based on typical salaries and employment rates for graduates from this faculty.
