Bachelor of Information Systems

Learn how to design, manage, and optimize digital systems that support business operations, decision-making, and innovation.

About Program

Information Systems is where technology meets business. This program equips students with skills in systems analysis, enterprise IT, data management, and digital transformation. You’ll learn how to apply technology to solve organizational problems, improve efficiency, and drive strategy. Graduates often serve as the bridge between developers and management — fluent in both tech and business language.

What You Will Learn

  • Fundamentals of system development & software architecture
  • Business process modeling and digital transformation
  • Enterprise databases and data analytics
  • IT project management and agile methodology
